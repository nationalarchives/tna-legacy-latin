Lesson 11: Fourth and fifth declension nouns; days of the week

Days of the week


dies septimane septimana, -e (f.) week

dies Lune Monday

 not Sunday!
 Note the feminine form
dies Martis Tuesday
dies Mercurii Wednesday
dies Iovis Thursday
dies Veneris Friday
dies Sabbati Saturday
dies Dominica Sunday
