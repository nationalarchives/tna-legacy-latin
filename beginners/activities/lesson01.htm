Activity 1 - First conjugation verbs - Drag and drop

How much do you remember? Try this activity to check what you’ve learnt and to help the learning process.

1. First conjugation verbs

Match the English verb with its Latin translation. Drag each Latin word from the list on the right hand side and place beside the correct English translation. If your answers are correct the tiles will automatically turn to red when you press submit.
